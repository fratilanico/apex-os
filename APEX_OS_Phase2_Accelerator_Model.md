# APEX OS - Phase 2 Accelerator Model
## "From Idea to GTM in 30 Days"

---

## Executive Summary

The Phase 2 Accelerator is a high-intensity, cohort-based program designed to transform validated ideas from APEX OS Academy into market-ready SaaS products. By leveraging AI-powered development tools and a proven 4-week sprint methodology, we compress the traditional 6-12 month startup journey into 30 days.

**Key Metrics:**
- Duration: 30 days per cohort
- Cohort Size: 10-15 founders
- Annual Cohorts: 4-5 per year
- Equity Stake: 15% per startup
- Target Portfolio: 40-60 startups over 3 years

### 3.2 Investment Vehicle Options

```
SAFE AGREEMENT TERMS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Standard SAFE Structure:
• Valuation Cap: $2M - $4M (based on traction & market)
• Discount Rate: 20% (if no cap triggered)
• No interest
• No maturity date
• Converts at next qualified financing or liquidity event

APEX OS SAFE Terms:
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Investment Amount:          $0 (program value)              │
│ Valuation Cap:              $2,000,000                      │
│ Discount:                   20%                             │
│ Pro-Rata Rights:            Yes                             │
│ Most Favored Nation:        Yes                             │
│ Post-Money SAFE:            Yes (YC standard)               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Example Conversion:
Startup raises $1M Seed at $5M pre-money valuation
• SAFE converts at cap: $2M valuation
• APEX receives: 15% equity ($300K value at $2M cap)
• If no cap: 20% discount on $5M = $4M effective
• APEX receives: 7.5% equity ($300K / $4M)
```
